About Me

I am a Senior Process Engineer with over 16 years of experience at EPROM-MIDOR REFINERY in Alexandria, Egypt. My expertise spans across various refinery units including Naphtha hydrotreating, Naphtha splitter, Naphtha reforming (platformer-CCR), naphtha isomerization (penex), kerosene merox, LPG merox, light ends treatment, and LPG recovery Units.

I hold a B.Sc. in Petrochemicals & Petroleum Refining Engineering from Suez Canal University and am currently pursuing an MBA at Alexandria University. My professional journey is marked by continuous learning and innovation, particularly in the areas of process optimization, data analysis, and AI applications in process engineering.

Beyond traditional engineering roles, I have developed a comprehensive suite of web applications specifically designed for chemical and process engineers. These tools, available at amsamms.github.io/web_apps/, include:

  • Units conversion tools
  • Gas properties calculations
  • Scatter plotting applications
  • Machine learning implementations

I am also actively involved in developing AI solutions for process engineering, including specialized GPT-based tools and machine learning models for process optimization.
